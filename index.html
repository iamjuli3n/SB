About

This is Thin Blue BBQ where law enforcement officers with a passion for service and BBQ come together to compete, cater, and create unforgettable culinary experiences. Our team is driven by a shared love for bold flavors and the art of smoking, using fresh, locally sourced ingredients to craft award-winning BBQ.

As a competition team, we bring our best to every event, blending tradition and innovation to deliver mouthwatering results. From slow-smoked brisket to perfectly seasoned ribs, every bite showcases our dedication to quality and flavor. Our expertise doesn’t stop at the competition table — we also offer catering services for events big and small. Whether it’s a family reunion, corporate gathering, or wedding, Thin Blue will bring the same championship-level BBQ to your special occasion.

Based in the heart of Michigan, we’re proud to serve our community and share our passion for BBQ. Let Thin Blue make your next event unforgettable with savory, smoky goodness and a side of warm hospitality.
